ABOUT

The Minstry follows its Vision and helps many to be empowered and be abled to live a life of a free man. We win many souls and baptize them for the Kingdom of God. Currently Rise Ministry has 3 churches and looking forward in God's Providence to have Church building for many Believers.

Keeping in view the need to train up the children, we conduct Sunday schools for the religious and ethical formation of the children of poor and vulnerable community. Well Trained teachers lead the 4 Acadmies in different parts of Punjab. Regular classes are conducted to teach the basic teaching of the Holy Bible. Teachers prepare the children for bible reading and worship as well. We train up the children to be an active part of the church and help them to live their lives according to the teachings of Jesus Christ. We often conduct monitoring visits to note the progress of all Sunday schools established at different areas of District Faisalabad – Pakistan.

In Women's Minsitry we train Widows to be skillful and try to teach them Sewing, Knitting, Embroidery and help many other things to make and sell in the market to earn while staying at home.

Please keep our ministry in your prayers and support the Cause for the good care of winning Souls, Helping Children & Widows

ON GOING PROJECTS

Winning Souls

Bringing people to God and introducing them to the Gospel. Along with our Team we are helping People to get closer to God and helping them to build personal Relationship with God. We do Door to Door Evangelism, Street Preaching, Child Evangelism and Lead Women Group.

Free Education and Food Project

Everyone deserves Education & Food. We provide help to those in need.

4 free Educational Academies are run under the supervision of educated and talented teachers. These academies are located in Madina Town, 109 Chak Jaranawala, Khanuwana and Mulkhanwala. Children are given free education along with all the educational needs and Equipment. They are also given Nutrient food to live a better life because these children come from either a broken or single parents families.

UP COMING PROJECTS

Church Building

Pakistan comes in the list of those countries which are dominated by other religion namely Islam. Christianity is less than 1.5% of the whole percentage. Sharing and spreading the Good News a bit Challenging but many want to listen the word of God particularly in the remote areas. We are constructing a church building but need your helping hand to finish the work so that we may bring all the people together and help them to be saved and enter in the kingdom of God.

Orphanage Home

Rise Ministry- Pakistan

One of the main focuses of the Rise Ministry is to save and protect the children and to provide them an Academic and Biblical knowledge. Millions of the children just spoil their lives because they have not been to school either they don't have parents, or they are abandoned because of the Poverty. We have a vision to build a Rescue Home for these children to be saved and have a happy home. We need your support for this greater cause.

Monthly Food Projects

For the Widows and Street Children

Our Ministry tries to provide a small food Packages to many widows and street to survive.
