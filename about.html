ABOUT

The Minstry follows its Vision and helps many to be empowered and be abled to live a life of a free man. We win many souls and baptize them for the Kingdom of God. Currently Rise Ministry has 3 churches and looking forward in God's Providence to have Church building for many Believers.

Keeping in view the need to train up the children, we conduct Sunday schools for the religious and ethical formation of the children of poor and vulnerable community. Well Trained teachers lead the 4 Acadmies in different parts of Punjab. Regular classes are conducted to teach the basic teaching of the Holy Bible. Teachers prepare the children for bible reading and worship as well. We train up the children to be an active part of the church and help them to live their lives according to the teachings of Jesus Christ. We often conduct monitoring visits to note the progress of all Sunday schools established at different areas of District Faisalabad – Pakistan.

In Women's Minsitry we train Widows to be skillful and try to teach them Sewing, Knitting, Embroidery and help many other things to make and sell in the market to earn while staying at home.

Please keep our ministry in your prayers and support the Cause for the good care of winning Souls, Helping Children & Widows
