ON GOING PROJECTS

Winning Souls

Bringing people to God and introducing them to the Gospel. Along with our Team we are helping People to get closer to God and helping them to build personal Relationship with God. We do Door to Door Evangelism, Street Preaching, Child Evangelism and Lead Women Group.

Free Education and Food Project

Everyone deserves Education & Food. We provide help to those in need.

4 free Educational Academies are run under the supervision of educated and talented teachers. These academies are located in Madina Town, 109 Chak Jaranawala, Khanuwana and Mulkhanwala. Children are given free education along with all the educational needs and Equipment. They are also given Nutrient food to live a better life because these children come from either a broken or single parents families.
